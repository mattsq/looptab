- **w=48 does:** a single **Оі=10** gives bandwidth {2,4,8} all 10/10-convergent, balanced (~0.50),

**Reading (per В§8 вҖ” the locality hypothesis FAILS to revive the edge; mechanistic attribution is a hypothesis, not isolated).**
- **Locality does NOT revive the loop's edge вҖ” if anything the reverse.** О”(loopвҲ’ff) is **negative at
  every bandwidth** on accuracy (all 0/10, p=.002); `ff_matched` is the **best arm across the entire
  ladder**. The direct test of M13's hypothesis (does locality revive the *joint-state coherence
  mechanism*?) is the trainability-clean О”(stepDSвҲ’decoupled_stepDS), which is **null at both the local
  and dense ends** (ns) and tiny in between вҖ” nothing like the ECA's +0.32вҖҰ+0.66. So a *local, non-CA*

- **De-emphasise the b=2 О”EM вҲ’0.80 вҖ” it is the LEAST informative cell.** b=2 is an *easy* task (ff acc
  0.999, near-solved) where **both** recurrent arms collapse vs ff (loop О”EM вҲ’0.80, decoupled вҲ’0.94) вҖ”
  i.e. "recurrence is pointless on an easy task," not a joint-state result. The load-bearing cells are
  the *hard* end (b=8, dense: ff acc 0.92/0.91, EM low for all arms) where the loop **still** fails to

- **Why ff tracks bandwidth: per-cell FAN-IN, and the bandwidthвҶ”depth CONFOUND (the key caveat).** ff
  acc runs 0.999 (b=2) вҶ’ 0.906 (dense). A shallow MLP is easyвҮ”each output cell has small fan-in, and
  fan-in вүҲ light-cone width вүҲ **bandwidth Г— convergence-depth**. The banded net changes **two** things
  vs the ECA at once: it drops the *uniform/translation-invariant* rule AND it collapses convergence
  depth (median 1вҖ“2 here vs the ECA's 2вҖ“3 with a tail to ~22). So M14 **cannot cleanly separate**
  "uniform local rule" from "deep convergence / wide light-cone" as the loop-edge ingredient вҖ” both are
  removed together. The ECA achieves large fan-in via *depth* (radius-1 rule iterated deep); the dense
  Hopfield via *bandwidth* at shallow depth; both are ff-hard, yet the loop won only on the ECA (M13).
  **Hypothesis (NOT isolated by M14):** the loop's edge needs the *iterated translation-invariant local
  rule* specifically вҖ” but "it's depth-composition, not the uniform rule" remains an equally-supported
  alternative this experiment does not rule out.
