# M24f — DONE (headline stands post-adversarial-review; one mechanism claim reworded off EM). The M24 cross-cell MIXER re-test carried to the airline `disruption` operator: M22's leg-2 null was a FLAT-ARCHITECTURE artifact — a cell-mixing architecture significantly beats feedforward (acc +0.041/+0.034, 8/0) where the flat loop ties it. It is the mixer ARCHITECTURE, not recurrence (M24e pattern). Two honest scope limits from the review: the win is raw per-cell accuracy EM-amplified (NOT coherence — `coherence_excess` Δ(mixer−ff) ≤ 0), and the mixing marginal does NOT "dwarf" the §9.2 legs on accuracy (an EM-floor artifact, corrected below). Closes the `mixed_converge`/`disruption` open thread (§11c) for `disruption`.

M22 evaluated `disruption` (a JOINT multi-output fixed point — `w` coupled flights under an integer THRESHOLD net over rotation-chain [LOCAL] + bank-clique [NON-LOCAL] coupling) with the FLAT `trm` and found leg-2 NULL/NEG: the loop had no coherence edge over `ff` (Δ(trm−ff) acc −0.025 0/8, EM −0.128 1/7 ns; ff the best arm). M24c/M24d then reframed the mixer's reach as **cross-cell DEPENDENCY, not locality** (it fires on local `converge` AND dense `hopfield`, inert on exchangeable `multi_parity`), and CLAUDE.md §11c flagged `disruption` (MED structure) as the untested case. This runs the LEAN M24 isolation on it — `trm_mixer` vs `trm_flat` vs `ff_matched`, standard train + deep supervision + RMSNorm + full BPTT, the cell-mixing operator the ONLY changed variable — plus the M24b mechanism ablation and the M24e tying control, all in one milestone. Configs `configs/experiments/m24f_disruption_{mixer_w24,mixer_w32,ablation_w24,untied_w24}.yaml`; 8 seeds, `num_threads=1` (mixer 3-D matmul). No model/generator code changed — configs + a screen script only, so every prior committed result is bit-identical.

**Stage 0 — the distractors=0 hardness screen (`scratchpad/screen_disruption_nodist.py`).** The mixer needs `in_features % out_features == 0`; with `n_banks=4` the per-flight feature block is `2+n_banks=6` wide, so `in_features = w·6` (144 @ w24, 192 @ w32) is divisible by `out_features = w` ONLY with `distractors: 0` (the M22 `distractors: 8` gives `152 % 24 = 8` ✗). Crucially, distractor columns are appended to X **after** `y` is built, so the TARGET is byte-identical to M22 — only ff/linear separability can shift. Screen confirms it stays ff-HARD and non-vacuous: **linear-ridge EM 0.231** (M22 ~0.23, unchanged), copy_frac 0.817, flips/row 4.39, em_baseline 0.040 — all matching M22. The w=32 minimal-PSD γ is confirmed **15** (γ=14 @ w24). So the only variable vs M22 is the distractor noise, which the flat-arm sanity Δ below controls for.

**⚠ ADVERSARIAL-REVIEW CORRECTION (the pre-review draft said the mixing marginal "DWARFS" leg-1/P1 on EM +0.498 vs +0.225/+0.197 — that is an EM-FLOOR ARTIFACT, the exact M24b trap).** On token-**accuracy** the ordering INVERTS: the mixing marginal Δ(mixer−flat) is **+0.047, the SMALLEST** of the three increments — leg-1 Δ(flat−decoupled) **+0.065** and P1 Δ(flat−untied) **+0.058** are both larger. The EM "dwarfs" ranking exists only because the mixer alone sits at acc≈0.99 where d(acc^24)/d(acc) is ~7× steeper than in the 0.88–0.95 band of the leg comparisons. So the mixing operator is NOT intrinsically a bigger contributor than joint-state/tying — all three are real, comparable accuracy increments (each 8/0 or 7/1). **The "distinct capability" claim survives, but on the clean untied evidence, not "dwarfs":** at matched budget an untied MIXER stack reaches acc 0.986 (train 0.998) while an untied NON-mixer stack (`untied_matched`) plateaus at acc 0.888 (train 0.893) — a **+0.098 accuracy** gap from the mixing operator alone, holding tying and depth fixed. The correct reading is: joint-state (+0.065), tying (+0.058), and mixing (+0.047) are all genuine, comparable per-cell-accuracy increments; the mixer's distinctiveness is that its increment is available to a NON-recurrent untied stack (it is architectural), and it STACKS on top of the flat loop's joint-state+tying (Δ(mixer−flat) +0.047 acc, 8/0), pushing per-cell acc into the near-solve regime where EM saturates. **The w32 ablation replicates this exactly** (accuracy ordering: mixing marginal +0.043 < leg-1 +0.109 < P1 +0.113 — mixing again the smallest), so the "does not dwarf on accuracy" correction is width-robust. The flat loop beats its own internal ablations (leg-1 + P1 reproduce on disruption, as M22 found) but stays at ff level on accuracy; only the cross-cell operator adds accuracy on top.

At BOTH widths the mixing ARCHITECTURE does the bulk of the work: a budget-matched UNTIED mixer (no weight tying) already beats ff by acc +0.034/+0.026 (8/0) / EM +0.406/+0.343 — capturing most of the +0.041/+0.034 acc headline — so "hard-solving = the cross-cell operator, not recurrence" holds. **But the TYING verdict is width-dependent, and w32 refines the M24e story:** at w24 tying is a small, NON-significant efficiency edge (ns vs both untied arms — the clean M24e/converge result), whereas at w32 the tied loop SIGNIFICANTLY beats BOTH the budget-matched untied stack (acc +0.009 / EM +0.136, 8/0) AND the 7.5×-param full-width ceiling (acc **+0.005** / EM +0.088, 8/0) — accuracy-first, as the EM figures are that few-point per-cell edge amplified over 32 cells. The cause is visible in the curve: at w32 the untied ceiling OVERFITS (train_acc 0.9999 → test EM 0.755 < tied 0.843), while weight-sharing appears to regularize the tied loop (train 0.996, test 0.843, per-seed EM 0.73–0.78 for the ceiling vs 0.76–0.94 tied). So on disruption w32, weight-tying is not merely efficiency — it CONSISTENTLY behaves as a generalization edge (P1 as regularization) that the extra untied capacity cannot buy, unlike M24e converge where the near-solve regime (all mixer arms acc ~0.998) left no overfitting room. (One width transition + one task, so this is an evidenced observation, not a proven law.) Net: the mixing OPERATOR is the dominant, width-robust factor; weight-tied recurrence is a small efficiency edge at low width that becomes a significant generalization edge at higher width where an untied stack overfits.

**Verdict (accuracy-led, post-adversarial-review).** The M22 disruption leg-2 NULL is a FLAT-ARCHITECTURE artifact: a cross-cell MIXING architecture significantly beats the param-matched feedforward — **Δ(mixer−ff) acc +0.041 / +0.034 (8/0, p=.008) at w24/w32** — while the flat loop does not (Δ(flat−ff) acc −0.006/−0.009). The mixing operator is a distinct capability (an untied mixer beats an untied non-mixer +0.098 acc at matched budget) and it is primarily the mixer ARCHITECTURE not recurrence (a budget-matched untied mixer already wins on acc +0.034/+0.026 8/0 at w24/w32) — with a **width-dependent tying refinement (both widths run):** weight-tying is a small/ns efficiency edge at w24 (the clean M24e result) but becomes SIGNIFICANT at w32 (tied beats even the 7.5× untied ceiling, acc **+0.005** / EM +0.088, 8/0 — stated accuracy-first, since the EM +0.088 is that same +0.005 per-cell edge amplified over 32 cells). This is CONSISTENT with weight-sharing regularizing where the untied stack overfits (ceiling train 1.0 → test EM 0.755 < tied 0.843, EM tightly plateaued 0.73–0.78 across all 8 seeds) — a single-width-transition observation, so read it as "recurrence is not entirely irrelevant on disruption at higher width, unlike M24e's near-solve converge (which left no overfit room)," not as a proven regularization law. **Two honest scope limits the adversarial review surfaced:** (i) the dramatic EM numbers (0.42→0.92, Δ +0.49) are the SAME +0.041 accuracy edge AMPLIFIED by whole-row EM — `coherence_excess` Δ(mixer−ff) is **−0.044 (w24) / +0.004 (w32, ns)** (mixer NOT more coherent than ff), and disruption is ~93% per-cell LINEAR (linear-ridge per-cell acc 0.926 @ w24 / 0.909 @ w32), so the win is raw per-cell accuracy on the last few points, EM-magnified, NOT a coherence edge (same honest framing as M24's converge correction); (ii) the mixing marginal does NOT "dwarf" the §9.2 legs — on accuracy it is comparable-to-smaller (+0.047 vs leg-1 +0.065, P1 +0.058); its distinctiveness is that it is architectural (available to a non-recurrent untied stack) and STACKS on the flat loop's joint-state+tying. This adds `disruption` (a domain-motivated MED-structure operator: LOCAL rotation chains + NON-LOCAL bank cliques) to the CROSS-CELL-COUPLED side of the M24c/M24d dividing line, alongside local `converge` and dense `hopfield`. Depth-extrapolation is untouched (fixed-point target; M1/M8/M21 out of scope).
