# M32 — decomposing M31's "shared-readout" attribution: channel-INDEPENDENCE is the larger slice (dominant when channels are many), the shared readout a modest second, token-mixing NOT the mechanism (net harmful on weather)

M31 resolved the M30 forecasting confound and re-attributed the mixer win from cross-variable
token-**mixing** to the **"shared-readout / channel-independent parameterization"** — but flagged
(its own Caveats / §11.3) that `Δ(nomix − ff)` **bundles three** things `trm_mixer_nomix` has and
`ff_matched` lacks and it never separated them: (i) the per-cell **shared** `Linear(latent,H)`
readout, (ii) **channel-independence** (each cell sees only its own variable), (iii) **per-variable
weight-sharing** of the channel MLP. M32 builds the controls to split it, each flipping ONE named
axis from `nomix` (plus the forced re-width that holding `trm_flat`'s budget requires — see Caveats;
so "single-axis + re-width," not "single-parameter"), all budget-matched to `trm_flat` (widths from
`scratchpad/m32/probe_budget.py`, within ~1.6%):

- `trm_mixer_nomix_unsharedro` — CI, **unshared** per-cell (block-diagonal) readout → isolates the
  shared readout on the CI body. `Δ(nomix − this)` = the readout's contribution.
- `trm_mixer_unsharedro` — mixing ON, **unshared** readout → readout isolation on the CD body (x-check).
- `trm_mixer_nomix_distinctw` — CI, per-cell **distinct** channel MLPs → isolates weight-sharing.

Additive within each cell: `Δ(nomix − ff) = Δ(nomix − nomix_unsharedro)[readout] + Δ(nomix_unsharedro
− ff)[CI-structure + …]`. All six cells (etth1/weather × {192,336,720}, 10 backtest blocks) reproduce
M31's four arms — bit-identically for `ff`/`nomix` everywhere and `trm_flat` in 5/6 cells, with the
one exception (`trm_flat` etth1-336, +0.0086) discussed in Caveats — so the decomposition is on the
*same* result (per-arm MSE below).

## The clean attribution — nomix vs `trm_flat` (the fair CD baseline, avoiding ff's overfit)

The `nomix − ff` headline is badly inflated by `ff_matched` **over-fitting** (M31's generalization
finding: the shallow joint MLP blows past persistence, spectacularly on weather — `trm_flat − ff`
alone is −0.92 of the −1.13 weather-h720 headline). The honest reference is `trm_flat` (a recurrent,
narrow, channel-DEPENDENT loop that generalizes fine). Against it, `nomix`'s win splits exactly into
**channel-independence** (`nomix_unsh − trm_flat`, both recurrent + unshared-RO, CI-per-cell vs
flat-CD body) + **shared readout** (`nomix − nomix_unsh`):

| cell | nomix − flat | = CI-structure | + readout | split (CI / RO) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| etth1-192 | −0.123 | −0.049 | −0.074 | 40% / 60% |
| etth1-336 | −0.115 | −0.018 | −0.097 | 15% / 85% |
| etth1-720 | −0.166 | −0.073 | −0.093 | 44% / 56% |
| weather-192 | −0.188 | −0.158 | −0.030 | 84% / 16% |
| weather-336 | −0.167 | −0.133 | −0.034 | 80% / 20% |
| weather-720 | −0.210 | −0.159 | −0.051 | 76% / 24% |

The **weather** splits (76–84% CI) are large and robust. The **etth1** splits divide small, noisy Δ's
and are NOT reliable to a percentage point: the CI-structure Δ's (−0.018 to −0.073) are baseline-
sensitive — the `trm_flat` etth1-336 value itself wobbles ~0.009 between M31 and M32 (Caveats), which
alone moves that cell's split 15%→9%. Read etth1 as "CI small, readout the more consistent axis," not
as a precise fraction. This contrast is also not a single-knob flip (it bundles readout-structure +
width with channel-independence — Caveats); the clean CD/CI flip is the mixing leg (Reading #1).

## Reading

1. **Token-mixing is not the mechanism; it does not help, and on weather it is net HARMFUL.**
   `Δ(mixer − nomix)` is mean-positive in all 6 cells (+0.03→+0.16). This is **decisive on weather**
   (10/0, 9/1, 10/0; p≤.021) — there, adding cross-variable flow at a held shared readout *costs*
   accuracy. On **etth1 it is NOT significant** (8/2, **6/4**, 8/2; p=0.11, 0.75, 0.11 — the h336 6/4
   is a coin-flip), so read etth1 as "no help," not "harmful." "Worsens with horizon" is **mean-only
   and weather-only**: the weather mean plateaus h336→h720 (+0.139→+0.141) and M31 flagged the weather
   mixing leg as non-monotone *by median* (0.06/0.14/0.12) — do not claim a monotone horizon trend. The
   firm, well-isolated claim is the NEGATIVE one (mixing is not the mechanism), reproducing M31's
   mixing leg exactly; "harmful" is the weather-only add-on. (The unshared-RO cross-check
   `Δ(mixer_unsh − nomix_unsh)` agrees on weather, 10/0–9/1; it flips small-negative on etth1, where at
   an unshared readout the arms are close — the readout, not mixing, is etth1's active axis.)

2. **The shared readout is a REAL, sign-consistent, but MODEST helper — not the majority ingredient
   M31's naming implied.** `Δ(nomix − nomix_unsh)` is negative in every cell (~0.03–0.10), decisive on
   weather (w-336 0/10 p=.002; w-192/720 1/9 p=.021), directionally consistent though weaker on etth1.
   So "shared readout helps" is vindicated — but it is one part, not the headline.

3. **Channel-independence (per-cell own-variable processing) is the larger structural ingredient ON
   THE MANY-CHANNEL DATASET; on etth1 it is small and noise-dominated.** Against the fair `trm_flat`
   baseline, the CI-structure term carries **76–84% on weather (M=21 variables)** — robust and large.
   On **etth1 (M=7) it is only 15–44%, and that number is NOT trustworthy to a percentage point**: the
   `trm_flat` baseline itself wobbles ~0.009 MSE between M31 and M32 at h336 (see Caveats), which alone
   swings the etth1-336 share 15%→9%; the etth1 CI-structure Δ's (−0.018 to −0.073) are small enough to
   be baseline-/noise-dominated. So the honest statement is **"CI-structure dominates when channels are
   many (weather); on few-channel etth1 the readout is the more consistent axis and CI is small."**
   Caveat that bounds even the weather number: the CI-structure contrast (`nomix_unsh − trm_flat`) is
   NOT a single-knob flip — it also changes readout *structure* and latent *width* (see Caveats); the
   only clean CD/CI flip is the mixing leg (#1), which agrees in direction (CD-via-mixing is worse).
   Every channel-dependent arm (mixing loop, flat loop, joint MLP) underperforms CI on weather — the
   DLinear/PatchTST channel-independence direction, at tiny scale.

4. **Weight-sharing is a third real-but-modest helper.** `Δ(nomix − distinctw)` is negative in all 6
   cells (~0.03–0.09, mostly 8–9/10) — giving each variable its own channel MLP at fixed budget forces
   it too narrow; sharing one MLP across variables is the better trade.

5. **The M31 headline magnitude is mostly an ff-overfit artifact; use `trm_flat`.** The raw `nomix −
   ff` grows −0.18→−1.13, but `trm_flat − ff` supplies MOST of it (weather-h720: −0.923 of −1.133, 81%;
   ff over-fits, worst on weather-h720 where its variance is ±1.8 from one block at MSE 6.60 — the same
   outlier M30/M31 flagged). So the three "ingredients" (readout / CI / weight-share) partition only the
   *minority residual* left after removing the ff gap — they are the *small, robust* Δ's above, not the
   big number. "Channel-independence leads" means it leads that residual (on weather), NOT that it
   explains most of the forecasting win over ff — that is largely ff being a bad arm.

## What this does to M31's claim

M31's compound name **"shared-readout / channel-independent parameterization" is vindicated as a real
COMPOUND — both parts contribute, same sign in every cell — but its EMPHASIS (readout named first) is
corrected.** On the many-channel dataset (weather) **channel-independence is the dominant slice
(76–84%)** and the readout a modest second; on few-channel etth1 the readout is the more consistent
axis and channel-independence is small/noise-dominated. So "it's the shared readout" is too specific,
but so would be "it's channel-independence" as a universal — the split is **dataset-dependent
(channel count)**, and the precise percentages are trustworthy only on weather (on etth1 they hinge on
a ~0.009 baseline wobble; read them as "small"). Two honesty bounds: (a) the CI-structure slice is
measured against `trm_flat`, and most of M31's raw `nomix − ff` number is actually the ff-overfit gap
(`trm_flat − ff`), not any named ingredient — so these are the ingredients of the *residual* after
removing ff's pathology, not of the full §4a headline; (b) the CI-structure contrast bundles
channel-independence with readout-structure and width (Caveats). M31's FIRM negative claim — "it is
NOT the cross-variable mixing operator" — is upheld and, on weather, strengthened (mixing is net
harmful; on etth1 it is merely "no help"). The synthetic constraint-coupled mixing wins (§11.2 #7–8:
Sudoku, `converge`/`hopfield`/`disruption`) are untouched; this is forecasting-only, and it sharpens
#8's dividing line — real forecasting sits on the channel-independent side, more so the more channels.

## Caveats / scope

- **The CI-structure term is a bundle, not a single flip — this bounds the headline.** `nomix_unsh −
  trm_flat` contrasts a per-cell channel-INDEPENDENT body against the flat channel-DEPENDENT loop, both
  recurrent + unshared readout — but they also differ in readout *structure* (per-cell block-diagonal
  vs one flat M×H map) and latent *width* (114–142 vs 64). So "CI-structure" means "the
  channel-independent per-cell parameterization as a whole" (own-variable input + per-cell tokenization
  + the freed width), not the information-restriction in strict isolation, and the 76–84% figure
  inherits that bundling. The *clean* single-flag CD/CI contrast that holds readout fixed is the MIXING
  leg (#1), which agrees in direction (CD-via-mixing is worse than CI) but is not used to compute the
  split; the flat-CD arms being worse still is consistent but not a one-knob flip. Likewise every
  ingredient delta bundles its named axis with the forced re-width that holds the budget (e.g. the
  readout flip also moves hidden 516→116) — the intended "cheap parameterization frees width"
  mechanism, but not a single-*parameter* change.
- **Recurrence is not separated from the CI-structure term.** All family arms are tied loops; `ff` is
  the only feedforward arm, and it over-fits, so an "ff → trm_flat" step conflates recurrence with
  generalization. Per M24e/M30 recurrence is inert on forecasting (untied ≈ tied), so we read the
  CI-structure term as architecture, not recurrence — but this run does not re-prove it.
- **The readout/CI split is dataset-dependent (channel count), not a universal constant.** Reported as
  two regimes (few-channel etth1 = readout-led; many-channel weather = CI-led), not a single ratio.
- **Carries M31's scope:** 2 datasets, lookback 96, tiny CPU models, 25 epochs, standardized MSE;
  paired sign-test p is INDICATIVE (nested backtest train sets) — the story is sign-consistency across
  6 cells + the two-regime split, not a single p. Benchmark scale (where CI is known to win outright)
  stays the open frontier (§11.3).
- **Reproduction is BIT-IDENTICAL for most shared arms, with one exception the "3-D-matmul" story does
  NOT cover (adversarial-review finding).** `ff_matched` and `trm_mixer_nomix` reproduce M31
  bit-identically in every cell; `trm_flat` is bit-identical in every cell **except etth1-336**;
  `trm_mixer` drifts only at the largest widths (weather-h720 ~2.6e-3, etth1-336 ~3.6e-3) — 3-D-matmul
  BLAS non-determinism, as M31 noted. **BUT `trm_flat` etth1-336 drifts +0.0086 (0.6909 → 0.6995)
  despite being a 2-D-matmul arm at `num_threads=1`** — an isolated, unexplained CPU non-determinism
  (its 2-D sibling `ff` reproduces exactly, so the 3-D cause does not apply). It is small (0.0086 MSE)
  and changes no qualitative conclusion, but because `trm_flat` is the baseline of the CI split it
  shifts the **etth1-336 CI share ~15% → ~9%** — i.e. the etth1 split is baseline-sensitive and should
  be read as noise-dominated (see Reading #3). New arms are additive + off-by-default (defaults
  byte-identical to the committed mixer, unit-guarded).

## Verdict

M31's forecasting attribution decomposes (against the fair `trm_flat` baseline, since most of the raw
`nomix − ff` number is ff over-fitting) as: **channel-independence (per-cell own-variable processing)
is the larger structural slice on the MANY-CHANNEL dataset (weather 76–84%) but small/noise-dominated
on few-channel etth1; the SHARED READOUT is a real, modest, sign-consistent second (~0.03–0.10 MSE),
and the more consistent axis on etth1; per-variable WEIGHT-SHARING a modest third; and token-MIXING is
NOT the mechanism — no help everywhere, net HARMFUL on weather (decisive, 10/0–9/1), merely "no help"
on etth1 (not significant).** M31's compound name is right that both readout and channel-independence
are real; its *emphasis* (readout first) is corrected toward channel-independence on the many-channel
side, but the split is dataset-dependent, not universal. M31's firm negative ("not mixing") is upheld
and strengthened on weather. Caveats that bound the headline: the CI slice is measured vs `trm_flat`
(the big `nomix − ff` number is largely ff overfit), and the CI contrast bundles channel-independence
with readout-structure + width. One reproduction wrinkle: `trm_flat` etth1-336 drifts +0.0086 (a 2-D
arm, not the 3-D cause), which makes the etth1 split baseline-sensitive. Canonical:
`m32_{etth1,weather}_h{192,336,720}_*`; runner + probe in `scratchpad/m32/`.
